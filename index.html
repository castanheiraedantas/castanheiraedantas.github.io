Our Story

Three decades of craft & conviction

Castanheira & Dantas was founded in 1991 with a single conviction: that great ceramics can only be made by great hands. Today, led by the second generation of the founding family, that conviction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our workshop is home to craftspeople who have been with us for over 20 years — and in several cases, whose children have followed in their footsteps. This continuity of knowledge, skill and passion is what makes our ceramics impossible to replicate.

Vases, bowls, jugs and decorative objects. Organic forms and rich glazes that bring warmth to any interior. Available in sizes from 15 cm to over 1 metre.
